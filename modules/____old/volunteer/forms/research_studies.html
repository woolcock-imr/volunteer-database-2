Listed here are all the Woolcock studies that are currently recruiting volunteers. Follow the link and you will be asked to answer some specific questions that will help researchers to identify if you are eligible to participate in this study.
RESPIRATORY RESEARCH
SLEEP RESEARCH
Help design an App for asthma?
We are looking for young people between 15 and 25 who have a doctor diagnosis of asthma to help design an App to improve asthma control.

How do COPD drugs really work?
We are looking for people between the ages of 40 and 80, who are current or ex-smokers, and have been diagnosed with COPD or emphysema by their doctor. You can help us understand how “long-acting bronchodilators” work so we can develop better treatments in the future.

Severe Asthma Register
The Severe Asthma Register is a central and secure record of people with severe asthma who are willing to be contacted as potentially suitable participants in the latest approved research in severe asthma.

Does inflammation in the airways change with age in asthma?
Are you a non-smoker who is over 40 and has asthma? You can help us investigate what causes airway inflammation in older people with asthma with a view to improving the design of drugs for asthma.

Can we monitor lung disease at home?
Do you have COPD? Have you been a smoker? Do you get short of breath or cough? We are looking for volunteers to test a new way of monitoring lung function at home, that could revolutionise long-term disease management in COPD

Bronchoscopy Study
We need asthmatics as well as healthy people for a research study into the mechanisms of asthma.

How is your COPD affected by sleep disturbances?
We are looking for volunteers to determine the relationship between breathing and sleep disturbances in COPD. This study involves consult with a lung physician, breathing assessments, and a sleep study.

Does your child use an inhaler? Are they starting to use it on their own?
If you have a child between the ages of 4 and 18 who uses an inhaler for asthma you are invited to take part in a research study about how and why children come to manage their own asthma from the parents’ perspective.

A new inhaler for mild asthma?
A new inhaler for mild asthma is being tested at the Woolcock Institute. Do you want to participate in this study?
Do you have vivid dreams and sometimes kick, punch, speak, shout or laugh while you are asleep?
We are investigating prolonged released Melatonin as a possible new and safe treatment to improve the symptoms of REM sleep behaviour disorder (RBD). If you are 40 years or older you may be able to participate in this study.

Sleep apnea & cognitive changes in older adults study
If you are aged between 50 and 80 and have noticed changes in your thinking and are not currently using CPAP therapy you may be able to help us with this study.

Insomnia Research
Do you have insomnia? Do you have problems getting to sleep or staying asleep? If you do we invite you to participate in this research study.

Healthy People with no Sleep Problems Wanted - Sydney based study
Do you sleep well? We need healthy, good sleeping volunteers to help us discover new tests that can help doctors better understand the impairments caused by the sleep disorder obstructive sleep apnea.

Sleep, Food Desirability and Metabolism study
We need males who are NOT involved in shift work to help us discover if night shift work can affect the desirability and metabolism of food due to changes in the timing of sleep.

Do you snore? Are you sleepy?
If you snore and think you may have sleep apnea you could be eligible to participate.
