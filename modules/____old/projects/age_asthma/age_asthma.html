Does inflammation in the airways change with age in asthma?

Are you over 40 and do you have asthma? Are you a non smoker?

Older people with asthma can have more problems with asthma than younger people do. This study is looking at the cells that cause airway inflammation in older people with asthma. It will have an impact on the way we design drugs for asthma. The study will involve 4 visits where we will perform breathing tests, blood tests & look down your airway with a camera on the end of a flexible tube using a standard method (bronchoscopy). Suitable participants will receive all study related procedures at no cost and travel expenses/time will be reimbursed for the participants from the Sydney area.
